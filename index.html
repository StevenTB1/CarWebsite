What is a Driveless Car?

A self-driving car is a type of vehicle that can navigate and drive itself without human input.

It uses sensors and other technology to detect and understand its surroundings, allowing it to move safely and efficiently without the need for a human driver. They can be used for transportation, delivery, or other applications.
